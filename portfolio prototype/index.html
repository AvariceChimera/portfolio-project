About Me

My name is Joshua Crabb and I am a Game Developer.
i enjoy working with verious aspects of games but try to focus my efforts on making them "feel good"
both visually and in terms of gameplay so i put my energy into design. from tweaking movement speeds or
making changes to assets (which in the projects below i mostly made myself) so they pop out more for players to see
i started coding as a sort of touch & go hobby only really learning bits and pieces. it wasn't until joning triOS that i really
sunk my teeth into languages and projects. I take a lot of pride in my work and typically need to be pulled
away lest i lose track of time and focus too long on one problem or aspect i feel that my main drive stems from that
need to polish, i want games to be as good as possible not just for players but for devs later down the line
the more polished a game is the less they'll need to fix it later. But with AAA gaming being what it is that level of dedication
is exceedingly rare unless you look at indie teams or solo devs, but that's enough waxing poetic! i am a passionate
individual and have a decerning eye trained to spot trends and the mind to work around them, if you want someone who
works well in a team look no further. now let's make a game worth playing
